WERATEDOGS Analysis

WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog. The account shares images of the dogs with a brief comment, then let their followers rate the dogs. These ratings almost always have a denominator of 10 with the numerator almost always above 10. For this project, WeRateDogs has downloaded their twitter archive, over 5000+ tweets which we'll be analysing. Additional data from Twitter API including each tweet's retweet count and favourite(like) count.
